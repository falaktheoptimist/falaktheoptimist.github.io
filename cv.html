Intern, Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO)

  • The overall goal of the internship was to design a kalman filter based FLL assisted PLL tracking loop for the GPS receiver. It makes the tracking loop more robust against ionospheric scintillations.
  • This involved understanding the working of the GPS receiver, more in depth study of the tracking loop and the effect of ionospheric scintillations on the signal. Also, learnt the working of kalman filter in detail.
  • The addition of kalman filter in the loop has the advantage of retaining lock even during the periods of signal outage thus avoiding the costly re-acquisition procedure.
